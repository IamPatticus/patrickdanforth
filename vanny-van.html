🔌 Wiring Notes

  • Panels parallel → combined current doubles, voltage stays put
  • 550V disconnect is overkill but properly rated for PV circuits
  • C65H-DC breaker: both poles wired in series for clean DC breaking
  • LiFePO4 battery has low-temp cutoff — won't charge below freezing (saves the cells)
  • 100A BMS means battery can deliver up to ~1280W continuous before it trips
  • Battery side = 12V, so 63A is generous headroom on a 30A max MPPT

📊 Solar Math

  • Array wattage: 200W (2× 100W)
  • Voc (parallel): 22.79V
  • Isc (parallel): 10.62A
  • Max charge current: 30A (MPPT limit)
  • Battery voltage: 12V nominal
  • Estimated daily yield: ~600-800Wh (sunny day)
