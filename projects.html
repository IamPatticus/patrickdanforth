Njord — Digital Photo Display

🛒 Planned

A solar-powered digital photo display for the van. Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W drives a 10.4" Waveshare QLED screen showing photos of our chickens and ducks. Off-grid capable with LiFePO4 battery and solar charging.

Build Notes: Originally planned as Njord sensor hub (GPS, environmental monitoring). Pivoted to photo display after realizing the hardware is perfect for a van-mounted slideshow. May add sensor logging later as Phase 2. Display will cycle through chicken and duck photos stored locally on USB or pulled from home server via Tailscale.
