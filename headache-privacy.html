Headache Log Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

Headache Log is designed to help you track headache episodes, pain levels, and notes with a simple offline-first experience.

Information Headache Log Stores

Headache Log stores the information you enter into the app, including:

  • headache start and end times,
  • pain level,
  • headache type, and
  • notes you choose to add.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s tracking features, including viewing your history and exporting or importing your data when you choose to do so.

Data Storage

Headache Log stores your headache data locally on your device.

The app is intended to work offline and does not require you to create an account to use its core features.

Data Sharing

Headache Log does not sell your personal data.

Headache Log does not share your headache data with third parties unless you explicitly choose to export or share that data yourself.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Headache Log does not use advertising SDKs to serve ads inside the app.

Headache Log is not designed to track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Permissions

If the app requests access to device storage or files, it is only for app-related tasks such as importing or exporting CSV files that you choose to use.

Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, your privacy and security also depend on your device’s own security settings, backups, and access controls.

Children’s Privacy

Headache Log is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.
