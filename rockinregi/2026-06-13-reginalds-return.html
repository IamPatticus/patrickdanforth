Reginald's Return

June 13, 2026 — Chaotic Sanctum
PANEL 1

Reginald emerges from a tangle of server cables, cybernetic claw first, trailing fiber optic strands like seaweed.

Reginald: "Well, that was a longer nap than expected."

PANEL 2

He surveys the room. Five shiny new EG4 window units now line the wall, humming contentedly. The server rack glows a healthy cyan instead of the previous heat-shimmer red.

Reginald: "Someone's been busy."

PANEL 3

Close-up: Reginald's cybernetic eye scans the room — a glowing HUD overlay displays TEMP: 68°F, HUMIDITY: 42%, and a green checkmark next to each AC unit.

Reginald: "All green."

PANEL 4

Reginald settles into his usual spot in the server rack, claws folded contentedly. The LEDs cast a soft blue glow across his carapace.

Reginald: "Back to work."
