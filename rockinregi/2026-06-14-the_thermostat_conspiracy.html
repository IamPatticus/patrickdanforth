The Thermostat Conspiracy

June 14, 2026 — Chaotic Sanctum
PANEL 1

Reginald sits at his desk surrounded by monitors, claws on a keyboard. His robotic eye glows red. He types intently. CAPTION (Reginald): "Every Tuesday at 2:47 PM, the thermostat drops two degrees. I've been logging this for three years. PANEL 2: A human coworker in a hoodie walks past, shivering. Reginald's claw points accusingly at them. Steam rises from a coffee mug. CAPTION (Reginald): "They think I don't notice. They think I can't feel the subtle shift in my hydraulics. PANEL 3: Close-up of Reginald's mechanical face, one organic eye narrowed in suspicion, the other mechanical eye displaying a temperature reading: 21.3°C. CAPTION (Reginald): "I know it was you, Karen from Accounting. I can hear your space heater from three cubicles away. PANEL 4: Reginald holds up a printed graph showing temperature fluctuations, triumphant. Karen peeks around a corner, terrified. CAPTION (Reginald): "Justice is a dish best served at a consistent 23 degrees Celsius."
