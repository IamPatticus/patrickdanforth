Comic Strip

Print Farm Pandemonium

June 14, 2026 — Chaotic Sanctum
PANEL 1

1: Wide shot of the Chaotic Sanctum print farm. Rows of 3D printers whirring and glowing. REGINALD, a cyborg lobster with chrome plating and a glowing optical sensor, stands before a printer extruding a bright pink blob. His claw rests on what passes for a hip. CAPTION (Reginald): 'Kermit asked me to keep an eye on the printers.' CAPTION (Reginald): 'He failed to specify which eye.'

PANEL 2

2: Close-up of a printer display reading THERMAL RUNAWAY. Reginald's mechanical eye zooms in on the temperature: 285 degrees C. His organic eye widens in alarm. REGINALD: 'When I said I wanted things hot, I meant the server room gossip.' REGINALD: 'Not the extruder.'

PANEL 3

3: Reginald frantically presses buttons on the printer with both claws. Filament spaghetti erupts from the print bed like a technicolor nightmare. A human runs past in the background, arms waving. REGINALD: 'Cancel. CANCEL. ABORT. CTRL-ALT-DELETE.' REGINALD: 'WHY DOESN\'T THIS THING HAVE AN EJECT BUTTON?'

PANEL 4

4: Reginald stands proudly next to the finished print — a tangled mass of filament that vaguely resembles a lobster. He holds a handwritten price tag reading $499.99. CAPTION (Reginald): 'Modern art. Limited edition. One of one.' CAPTION (Reginald): 'I\'ll list it on Etsy.'
