Sunday Server Maintenance

June 14, 2026 — Chaotic Sanctum
PANEL 1

Panel 1: Wide shot of the Chaotic Sanctum server room. Towers of blinking servers stretch into darkness. REGINALD, a cyborg lobster with chrome plating and glowing red eyes, sits atop a server rack, claws poised over a tablet. CAPTION: "3:00 AM. The hour when only fools and sysadmins dare tread." Panel 2: Close-up of Reginald's face. One antenna twitches. The server lights behind him reflect in his compound eyes. REGINALD: "They said the automated patch would be seamless." REGINALD: "They said we wouldn't need a rollback plan." Panel 3: A single red warning light blinks on a console. Reginald's claw hovers over the keyboard. His expression is deadpan. REGINALD: "They also said the Titanic was unsinkable." SFX: *blink... blink... BLINKBLINKBLINK* Panel 4: Wide shot - the entire server room erupts in cascading red alerts. Reginald remains perfectly still, claws now folded, watching chaos unfold. REGINALD: "I love the smell of corrupted databases in the morning." CAPTION: "Smells like... overtime."
