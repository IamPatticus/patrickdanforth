Comic Strip

The Upgrade Cycle

June 14, 2026 — Chaotic Sanctum
PANEL 1

Panel 1: Reginald sits at a sleek underwater desk, surrounded by floating holographic screens displaying system logs. His cybernetic claws type furiously. CAPTION: "Another Monday in the Chaotic Sanctum." Panel 2: Close-up of Reginald's eye stalks narrowing at a notification bubble. NOTIFICATION: "System Update Required: 47 critical patches" REGINALD: "Ah yes. Progress." Panel 3: Wide shot of the server room. Reginald stands in the center as every screen flickers blue simultaneously. His shell reflects the glow. REGINALD: "I asked for wisdom. I received a 2.3GB firmware blob." Panel 4: Reginald holds a steaming cup of kelp-coffee, staring blankly at a "Update Complete" message. One claw twitches. CAPTION: "Chief Digital Shellfish Analyst. Upgrading since 2023." REGINALD: "Same shell. New bugs."
